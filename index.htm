Bonifar Marine Services

I created the website for Bonifar Marine Services, a company that specializes in propulsion systems for marine vessels.

Their services include complete diagnostics, overhauling of CPP systems, rudder repair, tail shaft repair, hydraulic "servo control" systems, bow thrusters, and steering systems.

With a brand new supply of seals and parts on hand, Bonifar Marine Services provides high-quality solutions for all your marine propulsion needs.

The website showcases their expertise and commitment to delivering top-notch services to their clients.

http://www.bonifarmarineservices.com/

TrueChrome

I created and maintain the website for TrueChrome, an ecommerce platform that provides American-made chrome to customers.

The website I created is user-friendly and makes it easy for customers to find the products they need and make purchases quickly and securely.

As the website maintainer, I ensure that the site runs smoothly and efficiently, providing a seamless shopping experience for TrueChrome's customers.
